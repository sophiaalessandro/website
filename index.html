What I Do

Want to have a wedding in Sicily, but don't know where to begin? I'll be your guide! I will help you find a venue, and coordinate group events for your destination wedding.

Sicily: the largest island in the Mediterranean Sea. This pearl of Island seduces travellers with its glorious diversity of landscapes, brilliant waters and cultural treasures. Sicily is home to her majesty Mt. Etna, the highest and one of the most active volcanos in the world, enriching the soil to support extensive agriulture from vineyards which produce world class D.O.C. wines to fields of orchards.

Who I Am

Born in France to Sicilian parents who later migrated to the United States, I had the priviledge of being educated in the US while being raised in Sicily – travelling back and forth for most of my life until finally settling down 10 years ago in my hometown in the province of Catania. Having had the opportunity to travel extensively I can honestly say that Sicily is one of the most beautiful islands in the world. Having been an Event Planner for the past 16 years, it is with much enthusiasm and pride that I encourage those interested in a destination wedding to come to Sicily. English is my mother tongue and I am fluent in Italian and Sicilian dialect, as well as extremely knowledgable of all the wonders of this Island and able to guarantee a wedding experience that will leave you and your guest breathless...
